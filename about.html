After 12 intensive weeks of full-stack JavaScript, I was certain about two things. I knew for certain that this was the career path for me. Never had I been so passionate about something. It still feels wrong to call it "work". I also realized is that I had to leave the islands to truly follow my dream. That realization brought me to Los Angeles. I'm motivated to excel in this industry, collaborative, and always ready and able to pick up new skills as the needs of a project evolve. I'm incredibly excited to take my career to a new level as a web developer in a technological and cultural hub like LA, and look forward to exceeding your expectations. A hui hou!
